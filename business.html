Mountain Beach House Studio

I have been running a recording studio business for the past six years. After graduating from BYU in Music and Sound Recording Technology, I rented an office space in Downtown Provo and produced albums and records for many local artists. I have since moved to Los Angeles, Indiana, and now Utah, and all that time have operated my studio out of my home.

In addition to writing and recording my own music, I have also produced and recorded albums for Grizzly Goat, Likelihood, Taylor Carraway, Jaren McMillan (now of Vocal Point), and Heather McGregor, among others.
